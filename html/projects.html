MNIST Digit Classification

Explored different machine learning based approaches to classify handwritten digits provided by the MNIST data set. Personally applied K-means clustering and a 2-layer neural network to achieve an accuracy of 0.919 on the testing data.

Breast Cancer Analysis

Analyzed data obtained from cytological breast tissue samples to classify a mass as malignant or benign. Personally performed a K-nearest neighbors, as well as seven different classification trees, such as boosted trees, bagged trees and random forests.
